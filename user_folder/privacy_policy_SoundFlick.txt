SoundFlick Privacy Policy


Last Updated: January 14, 2026


SoundFlick ("the App") is a free app that displays advertisements.


DATA COLLECTION


The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data 

to external servers. All user preferences and imported sounds are 

stored locally on your device only.


ADVERTISING


This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)

- Usage data for ad personalization


You can learn more about Google's data practices at: 

https://policies.google.com/privacy


You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings 

under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.


PERMISSIONS


The app uses the following permissions:

- Vibration: For haptic feedback

- Internet: To display advertisements

- Foreground Service: To run gesture detection in background
